SAVR SIGN-UP GUIDE



Welcome !


To begin your signup, easily head to our signup page.




Our signup flow requires you to confirm your email when you do not use any third party authentication (e.g Google). Just fill in your details in the form, submit, and proceed to check your email for the confirmation link.



Click on the confirmation link and proceed to login.



After your first login, and until a subscription has been initiated, you will be required to select a subscription plan before using the dashboard. Please select a plan and follow the flow to easily secure your billing information with our payment provider.


When your payment is completed, please login to the SAVR dashboard again to start building.


You're all set.


Happy Building !
