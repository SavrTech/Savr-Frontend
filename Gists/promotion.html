Create Promotions



Promotions are in-app push notifications you can send at any time to users of all the builds of your application. You may have 1 active promotion at most per project. All users of any of your builds would be notified with the promotion while the promotion is active.


To create a new promotion, follow these steps.


1- Click on the project from the general dashboard. You will be routed to the project dashboard.



2- Set the list option to Promotions to reveal the Add promotion button.




3- Click on the Add promotion button to reveal the create promotion overlay.



The interface would require you to insert a Title and Message which will appear as the title and body respectively of the push notification.
