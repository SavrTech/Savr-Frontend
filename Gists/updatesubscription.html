Update Subscription



As shown on the pricing page, you may choose between the starter or pro subscription plans.



To upgrade your subscription plan, follow these steps.



1- login to your SAVR dashboard and navigate to the settings page from the options on the top right dropdown.




2- On your settings page, click on the update subscription button. This will let you select a new pricing option on the pricing page.



Your subscription will automatically be updated after proceeding with the selection. For upgrades, we apply the credit from your previous subscription so you will immediately be debited the balance for the selected plan. Refer to the confirmation email for more information.


For downgrades, we prorate the expended amount in your current billing cycle and credit you the amount that will not be spent for the rest of the cycle. The credited amount would be used in your next subscription payment.


Please note : If your projects exceed the project limit for your selected subscription plan, you will be required to meet with the requirements of the selected plan by deleting some projects before using the dashboard.
